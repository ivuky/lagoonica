What we offer

Everything you need
for a perfect stay

Two fully equipped apartments by the Adriatic — each with its own terrace, sea views and everything you need to feel at home.

Seafront Location

Both apartments sit in the first row by the sea — step outside and the Adriatic is right there. No roads, no distance, just water.

Private Balcony

A large balcony with an unobstructed view of the open sea. The perfect spot for morning coffee or watching the sunset with a glass of wine.

Fully Equipped

Air conditioning, full kitchen, WiFi, Smart TV and fresh linen — everything is included so you can arrive and relax from the first moment.

Free parking

A private parking spot is included with every booking — no searching, no fees. Pull up and head straight to the sea.

Pet Friendly

Your four-legged companions are welcome too. Bring them along and let them enjoy the sea breeze and open space just as much as you do.

Welcoming Gift

Every stay begins with a small welcome from us — local wine, homemade treats and fresh fruit waiting for you on arrival.
